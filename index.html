Espresso


Espresso is a coffee-brewing method in which a small amount of nearly boiling water is forced under pressure through finely ground coffee beans.

Affogota


An affogato known in full in Italian as (gelato) affogato al caffè is an Italian coffee-based dessert.

Latte


Caffè latte often shortened to just latte in English, is a coffee drink of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk, traditionally served in a a glass

Frappe


A frappé coffee, cold coffee, Greek frappé, or just frappé is a Greek iced coffee drink generally made from spray-dried instant coffee

Americano


americano or American, is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso shot with hot water at a 1:3 to 1:4 ratio, resulting in a drink that retains the complex flavors of espresso.
